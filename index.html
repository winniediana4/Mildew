BAR AND RESTAURANT

Mildew is a bar and restaurant situated along Kabiria,Kwa Maji, and has been up and running for the past nine years.

ABOUT US

We specialise in providing good and affordable services to our customers and potential clients while making sure they are comfortable enough to keep coming back.

We try to provide all the services that our customers might need, in that they dont have to stress or strain in looking for other related services elsewhere. We also make it our business to make sure they get the best customer service for them to feel at home.

We pride ourselves in providing the home away from home experience.
